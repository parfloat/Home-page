Overview

Ganesh Gopalakrishnan is Professor of Computer Science at the University of Utah. His Utah homepage emphasizes formal methods for HPC, correctness of GPU software, floating-point reasoning, and dependable scientific computing.

This managed draft is based on the current public Utah homepage at users.cs.utah.edu/~ganesh and is intended to become the maintained public destination that the Utah-hosted page can later redirect to.
